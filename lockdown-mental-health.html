Overview

The COVID-19 pandemic brought significant challenges worldwide, particularly to mental health. This project analyzes the results of a global survey conducted during the lockdown period, uncovering insights about the psychological impact on individuals across various demographics.

Objectives

  • Understand the mental health impact of COVID-19 lockdowns.
  • Analyze responses from over 1,500 participants globally.
  • Identify trends based on age, gender, and location.

Approach

The survey data was collected via an online form and analyzed using Python and Tableau. Key insights were visualized to communicate the results effectively.

Key Findings

  • Over 70% of participants reported increased anxiety levels during the lockdown.
  • Younger individuals (ages 18-30) reported higher stress compared to older demographics.
  • Participants from urban areas experienced greater emotional strain than those in rural areas.
